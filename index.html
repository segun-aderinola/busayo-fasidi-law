Immigration Status and Visa Processing

At Olubusayo Fasidi PC, we have a deep understanding of immigration law and can help you
navigate through the complexities of your case. Our goal is to ensure that you achieve your

Immigration Status and Visa Processing Services
Legal Actions and Appeals Services
Adjustment of Status
Eligibility assessments, document preparation, and representation. Fast-track your eligibility & citizenship with our support.
Visa Extensions or Renewals
Accurate, efficient processing of your visa extension or renewal documents. Avoid unnecessary delays and complications.
Citizenship Application and Naturalization
Even with a denial from the Board of Immigration Appeals, our attorneys can take your case to federal court for review. We have experience in handling appeals at all levels of the federal court system.
Student Visas
We handle all categories of student visas - F1, J-1, M-1. Prepare your application, manage deadlines, and more with our support. 
Non-Immigrant Visas
We help with the processing of both the K-1 visa for fiancé(e)s of U.S. citizens, and the K-3 visa for the spouses of U.S. citizens.
Fiance(e) Visas
We help with the processing of both the K-1 visa for fiancé(e)s of U.S. citizens, and the K-3 visa for the spouses of U.S. citizens.
Consular Processing
We will assist you throughout the consular processing phase, from preparing your documents to scheduling your visa interview and finalizing your case. 
Immigration Court Representation
From deportation defense to bond hearings to cancellation of removal, we provide comprehensive representation in immigration court proceedings.
Board of Immigration Appeals (BIA)
If your case has been denied by an immigration judge, Our US immigration lawyers can represent you in front of the Board of Immigration Appeals to appeal the decision.
Federal Court Appeal
Even with a denial from the Board of Immigration Appeals, our attorneys can take your case to to federal court for review. We have experienced many in handling appeals at all levels of federal court system.
Motion to Reopen or Reconcile
If you believe that your case was unfairly denied or closed, our immigration attorneys can file a Motion to Reopen or Motion to Reconsider with the appropriate government agency.
Petitions to Review
Have your case reviewed by a higher court with our help. We can file a petition for review to challenge the decision made by USCIS or the Board of Immigration Appeals.
Waivers
We assist in filing waivers for various immigration-related issues, including unlawful presence, fraud or misrepresentation criminal grounds of inadmissibility, and more.
Response to request for evidence or intent to deny
If you receive a request for evidence or intent to deny from USCIS, we can help you respond with a strong and persuasive argument to support your case.
VAWA (Violence Against Women Act)
We provide immigration services to individuals who have been victims of domestic violence, abuse, or those crimes and are seeking protection under the Violence Against Women Act.
Asylum
For individuals who have filed their home country due to fear of persecution, we can help with the asylum application process and represent you in court proceedings.
Deportation Proceedings
If you are facing deportation or have received a Notice to Appear, our immigration lawyers can provide strategic and aggressive defense to fight against removal from the United States.
