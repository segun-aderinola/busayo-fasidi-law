About us

Olubusayo Fasidi PC 

Olubusayo Fasidi PC offers extensive immigration services, including visa

applications, green card petitions, naturalization, and deportation defense. Your

legal goals are our priority.

Olubusayo Fasidi PC.

Olubusayo Fasidi PC is led by Attorney Olubusayo Fasidi, who has had a successful 14+
years career stint in US immigration law, and 6+ years in Texas uncontested
and collaborative divorce law.
Throughout her career, Attorney Fasidi has helped clients with all types of immigration cases, including family-based visas, work visas, asylum, and more.
In addition to her experience in immigration law, Ms. Fasidi is a trained mediator with a Master’s in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Houston Baptist University.
The teams and partnerships behind Olubusayo Fasidi PC are equally as qualified and experienced. We occasionally work with a network of other attorneys, paralegals, support staff, and more to make sure that every client we serve gets the best possible outcome.
Olubusayo Fasidi PC is a Houston-based U.S. immigration law firm committed to providing
comprehensive immigration services to individuals and families throughout the United States and across the world. 
Our services include visa applications, green card petitions, naturalization processes, and deportation defense. We also offer legal advice and representation for business immigration matters
such as work visas and employment-based green cards.
